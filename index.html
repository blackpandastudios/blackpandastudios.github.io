Our Latest Project

VR Locomotion Essentials - Version 3

Provides intuitive solutions to VR locomotion/Movement

Seamless integration into existing projects!

Our custom editor makes it painless to integrate all the required scripts onto your existing VR rig.

Real-time locomotion swapping!

Real-time locomotion swapping can be achieved by calling a single method! Enabling you to give back the choice of locomotion to the player!

VR automatic height adjustment

Automatically handles height adjustment in VR movement! no more walking through walls!

Supports Vive, Oculus & WMR Headset!

We have full support for the HTC Vive, Oculus Rift and Windows Mixed Reality headsets!

Run-In-Place locomotion

Simply hold in the activation button and begin walking or running in place to move through the scene!

Arm-Swing locomotion

Simply hold the activation button and begin swinging your arms in a walking motion!

Touch-pad Based Locomotion

As seen in games like Onward, use the left (or right) touch-pad as a 'thumb-stick' and move your thumb in the direction you wish to move. Press down to sprint.

Climbing, Pulling, Swimming, Skiing

And we also handles Climbing, Pulling yourself anywhere and everywhere, Swimming and Skiing via gesture recognition and more coming soon!
